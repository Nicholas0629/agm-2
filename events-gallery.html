Events at Swinburne Cinema

Swinburne Popcorn Run


Inspired by Sonic the Hedgehog 3, Swinburne Cinemas has started
its fifth Popcorn Run. All fitness levels can participate in
the 5km, 10km, and 21km run categories offered by the event,
which will take place on December 1, 2024, at
Swinburne University of Technology in Kuching.

Themed experiences await participants, such
as exhilarating areas modeled after Sonic's speed,
entertaining obstacle courses, and captivating water
features. Attendees will get exclusive medals and
T-shirts with Sonic themes, meet and greet Sonic
cosplayers, and have access to unlimited popcorn.

Swinburne Play Events


At Vivacity mall, Swinburne Play Fest 2024 brought families
together for an unforgettable carnival-themed event that
promoted quality time spent together. It included fun
events like character meet-and-greets with Garfield,
ransformers, and Spider-Man, as well as workshops.

Applecrumby, Vico, Air Selangor, and Honor were among the
major sponsors who provided interactive booths, prize
distribution, and family-friendly events. With imaginative
workshops, vibrant face painting, and caricatures, parents
and children jumped right in, turning Swinburne Play Fest
2024 into a flurry of laughter and camaraderie.
