Experience


Welcome to SkyBox, a brand-new way for you and your loved ones to watch the newest blockbuster films. SkyBox, a new way for moviegoers to enjoy their film from an exclusive viewing deck, is situated on the upper level of the theater.

With fully electronic recliner seats arranged to comfortably fit four adults in a row, this raised terrace offers privacy and comfort. SkyBox has a dedicated surround sound system to provide the best possible coverage in the room, even though it uses the same screen as the main hall.

With SkyBox, enjoy a more private movie-watching experience right now.
