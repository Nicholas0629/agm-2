Cinema LED Technology from ONYX With a futuristic LED theater display and ultra-sharp high definition content, Onyx, powered by innovation at Samsung Electronics, is set to revolutionize the traditional movie-going experience. With a maximum brightness of 146fL (foot-lambert), it is the first High Dynamic Range (HDR) LED theater display in the world that complies with the Digital Cinema Initiatives (DCI). This allows the screen to capture even the purest whites and deepest blacks with an almost infinite contrast ratio.
